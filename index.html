The UX Capsule is a comprehensive digital archive dedicated to preserving and providing access to a wide array of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design resources. Spanning from the 1980s to the present, it aims to serve as a valuable resource for students, professionals, and enthusiasts interested in the evolution and current practices of UX/UI design.
Our archive is meticulously categorized by decade, topic (such as research, design principles, case studies), and type of resource (articles, videos, templates, etc.). We've designed an intuitive navigation system that allows users to easily browse through historical trends or dive deep into specific UX/UI subjects.
The capsule encompasses a broad spectrum of resources, including but not limited to scholarly articles, industry research papers, design templates and tools, in-depth case studies, instructional video tutorials, and legacy software tools. We aim to cover every aspect of UX/UI design, from theoretical foundations to practical applications.
